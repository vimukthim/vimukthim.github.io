Raw Material
Write-Off
Reduction

Achieving Target 54% Raw Material Cost (RMC) through systematic identification and elimination of write-off drivers across overshipments, BOM errors, ordering mistakes, and development issues.

This initiative addressed a critical cost leakage in raw material write-offs, which had peaked at $2.5M in 2022. I led a cross-functional A3 transformation focused on root cause elimination across procurement, merchandise development, and production interfaces. By deploying structured analytics and governance mechanisms, we identified systemic drivers including supplier over-shipments, BOM inaccuracies, ordering deviations, and development inefficiencies. A six-pillar strategy was implemented, combining supplier performance management, BOM validation controls, demand alignment, and process standardization supported by Power BI-driven visibility. The outcome was a sustained reduction exceeding 73% in write-offs, with 2024 performance tracking near target levels.

Strategy
Six Strategic Pillars
01
Reduce Overshipment Within Tolerance
  • PO/Supplier wise overshipment tracking via Power BI Dashboard
  • Max utilisation of over-deliveries (repeat & non-repeat colors / materials)
  • Adjust POs at ex-mill for foreign supplier for MRP visibility
  • Reduce BOM wastage % for repeat over-deliver articles
02
Eliminate BOM Mistakes
  • PLM integration for BOM automation & revalidation
  • Identify common BOM mistakes with internal teams
  • Continue BOM scorecard monitoring
  • Build New styles consumption timeline to adjust POs in Buy T&A
03
Eliminate Ordering Mistakes
  • Identify common mistakes & develop solutions
  • Create separate POD for Labels
  • Demand recovery plan for possible WO
  • Stock lot products & redye project activation
04
Fix Development Issues
  • KPI for External & Internal Teams + cost recovery
  • Stock lot, additional order & redye project
  • Common dev issue identification with dev houses
05
Reduce MOQ Order for Material Overconsumption
  • Identify common articles, update consumption in system
  • Additional analysis for over-consume items
  • Accessories cross-grade/SKU reduction
  • Customer material level deep-dive
06
Fix Sales Order Mistakes
